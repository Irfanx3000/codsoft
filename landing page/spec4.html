Pagani Huayra Roadster

The Pagani Huayra Roadster is an ultra-exclusive and high-performance supercar produced by the Italian manufacturer Pagani Automobili.

Price: $3,400,000 (INR:28 crores)

Performance :

  • Engine: 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine
  • Horsepower: 764 hp
  • Torque: 900nm (740lb-ft)
  • Top speed: 210mph (338 km/h)
