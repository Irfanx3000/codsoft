Mclaren P1

The McLaren P1 is a limited-production plug-in hybrid sports car produced by British automobile manufacturer McLaren Automotive.

Price: $1.15 million (INR:8.73 crores)

Performance :

  • Engine: V8 3.8L
  • Horsepower: 903 hp
  • Torque: 900nm (644lb-ft)
  • Top speed: 217mph (350 km/h)
